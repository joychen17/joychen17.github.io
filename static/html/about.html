Hi, I am Joy.

I received my bachelor's degree in Engineering Science from the University of Toronto where I continued to earn my masters degree with the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My masters research work introduced me to the world of compilers and I have been doing work in the area since. I first joined a Toronto based startup, Tenstorrent, where I took on several projects within the compiler team as well as the machine learning research team. Then, I relocated to Silicon Valley and joined Qualcomm. I now work on the compiler toolchains team at Qualcomm where I support the hardware team in the next generation of Arm based CPU.

My other interests include drawing, singing, baking, and jumping rope. Take a look at some of my creations!
